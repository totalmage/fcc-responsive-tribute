Called to serve and to heal

Russell M. Nelson is perhaps one of the most important men of his time. Having contributed greatly to the field of heart surgeries and being called as the current leader of one of the most rapidly growing religions, progress has been a part of him from the beginning and it does not look like he will stop soon.

Life Facts

  • Built the first heart-lung bypass machine in order to perform open-heart surgeries
  • Performed nearly 7,000 surgeries before his call as an apostle across least 4 continents
  • Gave 35 years of service as an Apostle for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints
  • He met his wife will performing theater

To read more information about Russell M. Nelson you may read the article on theChurchOfJesusChrist.org.
